04What the Lease Has to Have

Long deals need clear rules. These 4 lease terms are non-negotiable; if any are missing, neither side should sign.

1. SNDA from your lender? Three-way promise that protects the deal if anything ever happens to your mortgage. Without it, we walk.
2. Equipment ownership clarity The chargers belong to Future Tech 9000, recorded with the state. Not real estate.
3. Lease recorded at the county A short summary filed at Clark County. Public proof of our rights. $45 fee.
4. Cure rights? If either side messes up, the other gets notice and a chance to fix it. 30 days for money, 60+ for other.
